Ticketmaster Analysis: Rock Concerts

Welcome to our website! For our Cloud Computing class for our Masters in Business Analytics program at Cal Poly, we created this website to highlight our skills in AWS data analytics. In this project, we analyze data from Ticketmaster regarding rock concerts. Our analysis is updated daily by pulling from the Ticketmaster API.

By: Alexa Hildebrand, Eva Murdock, Daniel Hill, Toby Guenthner, Fernando Fonseca
